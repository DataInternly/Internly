Your legal protection

Learning Agreement support

The Learning Agreement (LA) is the international equivalent of the Dutch POK — a binding document between you, your institution, and your host employer. Internly manages the entire signing flow digitally.

📄 Learning Agreement
Internly Worldwide
👤Student — Lena de Vries ✓ Signed
🏢Employer — Vandaag Agency ✓ Signed
🎓Institution — HvA Amsterdam ⏳ Pending
2 of 3 signatures complete
All parties receive a signed copy by email.

What the LA covers

  • Internship duration and weekly hours
  • Learning objectives and evaluation criteria
  • Supervision arrangements at both sides
  • Credit recognition and academic integration
  • Erasmus+ compatibility (when applicable)
Erasmus+ compatible — but not required

Whether you're on an Erasmus+ grant, a bilateral agreement, or organising your internship independently, Internly's LA flow works for all three.

No. Any higher education institution can register and manage Learning Agreements through Internly. Erasmus+ eligibility is handled separately by your institution.
